A homepage built from ascent, sacred hierarchy, and stepped form. The visual system follows your existing language: deep red type, pale ground, vertical motion, and architectural rhythm inspired by the Historic Ensemble of the Potala Palace in Lhasa.

Concept

Ascending as Architecture

The homepage is organized like the palace itself. Wide base, stepped movement, elevated core. The structure turns verticality into navigation so the page already feels like a climb.

Identity

Form, Ritual, Height

The type is oversized and directional, echoing the expressive Tibetan-inspired forms in your poster work. The small ladder mark stays as a signature element, while stair blocks translate the visual language of walls, terraces, and ascent into web layout.

From city to mountain, from wall to summit, the page rises the same way the palace rises.

Historic Ensemble

The homepage can link outward to Potala Palace, Jokhang Temple, and Norbulingka as one connected UNESCO system instead of treating the site as a single image.
